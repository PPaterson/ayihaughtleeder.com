Regulations & Information
Passenger Conduct

Passengers would facilitate traffic by distinctly hailing the tram at stopping place, having correct fare ready for conductor, and promptly notifying conductor of desired destination.

Intending passengers must not board or alight from a moving car. Passengers must not interfere with any part of the car equipment.

Smoking is not permitted in enclosed cars. Spitting on cars is strictly prohibited.

Lost Property

All articles found in the tram must be handed to the conductor. Passengers losing any article whilst travelling on trams are requested to make application for the return of same at the Commission's office.
