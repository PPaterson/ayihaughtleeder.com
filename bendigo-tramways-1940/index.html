Regulations & Information

The Commission accepts no responsibility for any delay which may occur from any cause whatsoever, and reserves to itself the right to alter this time-table without notice.

Passengers Would Facilitate Traffic By—

  • Distinctly hailing tram at stopping place.
  • Waiting for passengers to alight before boarding tram.
  • Not standing at entrance to tram.
  • Having correct fare ready for conductor.
  • Promptly and clearly notifying conductor of desired destination.
  • Always keeping check ready for inspection throughout journey.
  • Destroying check after leaving tram.
  • Assisting the motorman and conductors in the observance of the by-laws and regulations.
  • Immediately reporting to the office any complaints against the Commission's servants.

Issued subject to the By-laws.

Lost Property

All articles found in the tram must be handed to the conductor. Passengers losing any article whilst travelling on trams are requested to make application for the return of same at the Commission's office.
