⚠ Malaysia Domestic Online — 1% (Root Cause Identified)
77% of MY domestic bookings are AirAsia, which requires Instant Purchase ticketing — incompatible with the current Asia approval workflow (up to three approval layers). AirAsia & Firefly are available in Cytric but can't be switched on without moving to FCM Pre Trip Approve, which would bypass the OBT approval step. This is a policy decision for Aurecon.
